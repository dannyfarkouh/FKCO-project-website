WELCOME!

The Camille Ohan Branch is a growing family of karate schools all under the direction of Shihan Camille Ohan, 7th degree black belt.

We are a member of the WKO (World Karate Organization) in Japan, which spans 9 regions of the world: Africa, Asia, Central America, Europe, Middle East, North America, Russia, South America and South Pacific.

Join us! Learn in a pleasant family atmosphere, where discipline and respect prevail without exception. We offer karate courses for juniors, teens and adults, as well as the Mini-Karate (ages 5 and 6). Our program is structured to provide balanced mind and body training.
